The CS Forever, an executive hotel welcomes business & leisure travellers for experiencing the Heritage of Indian hospitality .Here you will get world class accommodation within your budget.

The Cs Forever is located in heart of Pune city, following its legacy from Hotel Tushar, already a symbol of True Indian Hospitality since 1984. It provides an easy access to all major destinations in & around the Pune city. Whether you are at the business trip or a pleasure tour, you will be offered high level of service and hospitality as well as wide range of amenities and upscale facilities creating a warm inviting environment.

The CS Forever pampers the style conscious & upbeat traveller with its personal services and premium in-room amenities. We present you the mid-scale business and leisure hotel with star facilities that uplift your spirit spa at the end of long day. This stylish business hotel with fresh & bright interiors refreshes you with its spirited environment. Here friendly smiles & pleasant atmosphere go hand in hand with professional service.

We continue to enhance our facilities & the level of our efficiency as we believe in the great Indian tradition “Athithi Devo Bhava” meaning the customer is worshipped as God.
